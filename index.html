Win the Day Racing was founed by life long horse owner and trainer Matt Larsen and his wife Promise Larsen in 2019. Win the Day Racing is located in Honeyville, Utah and has a rich history of breeding, training, and racing quarter horses in Utah, Idaho, Wyoming, Nevada, and California. Win the Day Racing is home to multiple race winning mares and geldings, with more on the way. Contact us at: winthedayracing@gmail.com
