Win The Day
Racing

Win the Day Racing was founded in 2019 by Matt and Promise Larsen of Honeyville, Utah. Matt has been breeding, raising, and racing horses for over 40 years including both flat track and cutter racing. During his racing career Matt has trained or helped or his father train many notable horses including Crown Apple, Go Melvin Go, Italian Princess, KJ Streaking Jill, Kono, Pendelton, Prime Cat, Senor Call, Shooting Sixes, Stormabash, and Silver Dollar Derby winner Streaking Chloe
