Kono (ID)


×

Idaho bred Mare by Dashair out of Italian Princess by Blushing Bug.

In 22 starts Kono earned 5 wins, 3 second place finishes, and 5 third place finishes. Earning $34,296 in her career.

Career Highlights

Track Record Holder at 330 Yards at Elko County Fair.

2nd in Elko County Fair Derby 2017.

5th in AQHA Distaff Challenge Championship (Gr. 1) 2018.

2 Wins at Los Alamitos Race Track.
